Model Instructions

The model is designed to predict whether a customer will buy shoes based on several factors such as age, gender, income, and shoe size. It utilizes a Random Forest Classifier from the scikit-learn library to perform the predictions.

These are the following factors whether a person will buy shoes:

  • Age: 18-70 years old
  • Gender: 0 for Male, 1 for Female
  • Income: 20,000 - 100,000 per year
  • Shoe Size: Typically around size 5 and above

The model is more likely to predict a purchase if:

  • The person is young (under 30) with an income over 40,000
  • The person has a high income (over 70,000)
  • The person is older (over 50) with an income over 60,000
